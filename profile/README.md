# ScarcePH

**Building commerce tools for modern reselling.**

ScarcePH is a technology-driven platform focused on streamlining sneaker and limited-item reselling operations through automation, commerce infrastructure, and AI-powered customer experiences.

## What We Build

### Commerce Infrastructure
Dedicated customer and admin portals, inventory and order management, product catalog administration, secure checkout workflows, and sales monitoring tools.

### AI Customer Experience
Conversational AI integrations for handling customer inquiries, product discovery, and support through meta messenger platforms.
